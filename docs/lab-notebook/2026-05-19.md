### Empirical pulse-width endpoints

Determined visually by stepping through candidate values and observing horn position.

| Endpoint | LED0_OFF (hex) | Ticks | Commanded pulse (nominal, at 25 MHz clock) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Minimum | 0x0066 | 102 | ~0.50 ms |
| Maximum | 0x01FF | 511 | ~2.50 ms |
| Center | 0x0133 | 307 | ~1.50 ms |

LED0_ON_H/L are both 0 for all commands.

Actual pulse widths differ from nominal due to PCA9685 oscillator running ~12% high (documented 2026-05-12). Will be reported in paper as measured values.

The two endpoints produce visible motion through approximately the full SG90 mechanical range without end-stop buzz. The exact angles are not measured for this experiment because the paper reports oscillation parameters as measured values, not setpoints.

### Mechanical durability check

10 oscillation cycles (1 second per direction, ~20 sec total) executed at the endpoints above. Observed:

- Heavy base stayed put
- Sensor remained attached to horn
- Wires stayed clear of swing arc
- No new servo noise
- Servo body did not shift on foam tape

Rig is mechanically validated for the planned protocol.
